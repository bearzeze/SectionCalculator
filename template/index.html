Get your cross-section dimension fast and accurate

Application for calculating cross-section and chosing standard dimension of the structural elements

About

This application provides you with the ability to calculate the necessary and adequate desired standard cross-section.

  • Precise and fast determination of required dimensions
  • Managing multiple variants of various loads
  • Easy way of navigating throughout the process

The application also allows for the selection of the best possible solution from among all available standard cross-sections. Optimization leads to the selection of a structure that can handle the same load with the same material but has the smallest possible cross-section. This leads to cost-cutting measures.
